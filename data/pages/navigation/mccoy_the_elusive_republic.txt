====McCoy, The Elusive Republic: Political Economy in Jeffersonian America====
=== Animating Questions/Argument: === 
**Question: ** How did the Jeffersonians reconcile republicanism with their increasingly commercial society? \\
In the development of the political economy there are two major ideas about the way the country ought to work -- the Madisonian vision still invested in the agrarian version of republicanism and the Hamiltonian vision directed towards further development.
