==== NEW INDIAN HISTORY ====

Various definitions swirl around, but this is generally understood to be histories that center the Indian experience rather than that of the European interloper. Notable (and often self-described) practitioners include [[navigation:blackhawk_violence_over_the_land|Ned Blackhawk]], [[navigation:white_the_middle_ground|Richard White]], [[navigation:brooks_our_beloved_kin|Lisa Brooks]], among many others.
