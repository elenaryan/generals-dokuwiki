====Willard Hurst, Law and the Conditions of Freedom in the Nineteenth-Century United States====
=== Animating Questions/Argument: === 
**Question:** How did American law change during the 19th century?\\
Hurst argues for two major periodizations:
  * 1800-1870 - This Hurst characterizes as a directed release of energy. The "political challenge of the social environment. Much of this is what we could call expansive development -- transportation, agriculture, credit, encouragement of industry.((Hurst, 52))
  * 1870-1900 - This, Hurst says, is characterized by a "preoccupation with the economic challenge of physical and social circumstances.
